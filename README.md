# OLA Ride Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Project

## Project Overview
This project focuses on extracting actionable business insights from ride-hailing data to improve operational efficiency and understand customer behavior. 
* The analysis is built on a synthesized dataset representing 1 month of ride data across 50 areas in Bengaluru city, containing a total of 100,000 records. 
* The data encompasses 19 critical columns, mapping out requirements like vehicle types, pickup/drop locations, wait times (V_TAT and C_TAT), and booking statuses.
* A 62% successful booking rate was established as the baseline to evaluate revenue metrics, ratings, and ride distances.

## Technical Stack
* **Database & Querying:** SQL (Views, Aggregations, Filtering)
* **Data Visualization & BI:** Power BI

## Business Problems Addressed
The project was divided into two main phases to answer core business questions:

### 1. SQL Data Exploration
SQL queries and views were developed to clean, filter, and extract specific data points, including:
* Calculating the total booking value for all successfully completed rides and retrieving rides paid specifically via UPI.
* Identifying high-value users by listing the top 5 customers who booked the highest number of rides.
* Evaluating operational metrics by finding the average ride distance and average customer rating for each vehicle type (e.g., Prime Sedan, Mini, eBike).
* Tracking service failures by counting rides cancelled by customers versus rides cancelled by drivers due to personal or car-related issues.

### 2. Power BI Dashboard Development
An interactive Power BI dashboard was constructed, segregating insights into five key business views: Overall, Vehicle Type, Revenue, Cancellation, and Ratings. 
* **Volume & Status:** Utilized time-series charts to track ride volume over time and doughnut charts to visualize the breakdown of booking statuses (Success, Cancelled, Incomplete).
* **Revenue Tracking:** Built stacked bar charts to display revenue generated by various payment methods, and leaderboards highlighting the top 5 customers by total booking value.
* **Cancellation Analysis:** Created bar charts detailing specific reasons for ride cancellations, ensuring customer cancellations remained under 7% and driver cancellations under 18%.
* **Rating Distribution:** Implemented scatter plots to compare customer versus driver ratings, and box plots to visualize the spread of driver ratings across different vehicle types.
